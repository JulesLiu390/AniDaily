# Comic Style Settings

## Art Style
- Style: Cel-shaded anime, clean lines, bright colors
- Tone: Bright and lively

## Language
- Dialogue: English
- Sound Effects: Japanese-style onomatopoeia

## Layout
- Direction: Vertical comic strip
- Panels per strip: 4-6 panels
- Bubbles: Circular speech bubbles

## Characters
- Proportion: Normal (5-7 heads tall)
- Expressions: Moderately exaggerated

## Mood
- Style: Light-hearted and humorous, campus/workplace daily life
- Pacing: Medium, balanced dialogue and action

## Scenes
- Background: Simplified but recognizable
- Setting: Modern
